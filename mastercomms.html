👻
Answer the Call
SCRIPT▼

"GUTTER GHOSTS, _________ speaking — how can I help you today?"
Fill in your name. Warm, confident, direct. Let them speak first.
2
👨🏻
Listen & Acknowledge
RESPOND▼

Three common client openings — acknowledge, assure, redirect:

If they ask if you offer a service
"Absolutely — that's one of our core services. You've called the right place. Let me grab a few details and we'll get your quote started right away."
If they ask for a quote
"Perfect — we handle [service] all the time across Durham Region. I'll take down your info and have our quoting specialist Sarah get that sent over to you today."
If they describe a problem
"That's something we deal with regularly — no worries at all. We'll get one of our crew out to sort it. Let me grab your details and we'll take care of it from here."
